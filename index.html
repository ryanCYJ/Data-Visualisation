Plane Crashes from 2000 to 2025

During 2000 to 2025 ...

There are a total of 810 recorded plane crashes.

42% of these crashes are commercial planes, 16% are military, and 42% are private / others.

There is a total of 17 midair collisions.

The deadliest year was 2000, with 1430 fatalities from 63 crashes.

The safest year was 2023, with only 102 fatalities from 4 crashes.

The deadliest single crash occurred in 2014, when a Malaysia Airlines plane was hit by a Russian missile midair, killing all 298 people onboard.
